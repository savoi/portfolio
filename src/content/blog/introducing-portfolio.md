Welcome to the newest iteration of my digital home. I wanted something fast, content-first, and friendly to maintain. Astro ticks all those boxes and more.

## Why Astro?

Astro's focus on shipping zero-JS by default means I can obsess over the writing and storytelling without pulling in heavy frameworks. Content collections keep everything typed and organized, while MDX (coming soon!) will let me mix prose with interactive demos.
